About me

Hi, I'm Álvaro Basanta. I'm a musician from Spain, graduated in guitar at the Royal Conservatory of Madrid. My love for cinema and music made me get very interested in film scoring, so I also make music for films, documentaries, and right now I'm working on the soundtrack of an audiobook (Me Gusta el Olor de tu Alma by Libertad Pelayo).
